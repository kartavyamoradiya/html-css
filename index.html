Welcome

Your summer house awaits! Book now today!

Gransäng is a small village tucked away in the Swedish heart of Småland. It is surrounded by forests and teaming with wildlife. Your hosts are Melissa and Marcus Richt, two Swedes and busy parents of two who enjoy travelling and photography. Gransäng is less than a 10 minute dive to the nearest town. There you will find all the ammenities you need including supermarkets, a wide range of restaurants and an enjoyable town square to take a stroll. The village is also 2 minutes from the major highway that will take you to Gothenburg, Stockholm or to the South. A perfect stop off if you're driving the entire Sweden!
